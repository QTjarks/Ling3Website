Language Myth 3

The media are ruining English

In every age, there are certain individuals who proclaim that the English language is being bastardised, the blame for this has shifted around through the years and has now landed with the media. These individuals believe that the media often mistreats grammar rules and because they reach such a broad audience, promote other to do the same. This, however, does not hold up factually. These individuals are practically denying that the English language evolves over time and the grammatical rules with it. These individuals are free to use the forms of English they believe to be right, but they should attempt to prevent the English language from evolving into new forms as language evolution is a simple necessity for a language to persist through the years.
