Assignment 5.1: Teaching Language

The goal for this exercise was for me to note down how i will teach language based on the material i explored in week 4.

After obtaining new information from my research and my personal experience I have been able to gain at my internships, I am still set on the idea of having students re-enact real-life scenarios to gain fluency in the English language instead of having them know the meaning of the words and have them be clueless on how to interact within scenarios they will undoubtedly encounter. The grammar translating method will be a part of the curriculum as to expand the students’ vocabulary yet will not take a primary role. Even if a student’s vocabulary might be extensive, it is vital that the full extent of that vocabulary can be utilised, which I believe can be achieved by exercising real-life scenario’s with the students to promote quick thinking and to create a sense of anticipation within the students so they can prepare for the response they might get from their conversational partner. Task-based learning can offer students an excellent way in which they can practice fluency within the language by giving them a (for example) limited amount of time. Communicative language learning is an excellent way to practice sentence building and fluency in one. This method will often employ unrehearsed exercises, this allows a student to both train their fluency within the language and promotes using the language creatively by requiring them to answer questions in a manner appropriate to the scenario overall. I believe that communicative language learning promotes the student’s ability to use the language creatively yet correctly, which allows them to freely use the language in almost any real-life scenario they might encounter.
